Legal Management System

The Legal Management System is designed to assist law firms and legal professionals in managing clients, lawsuits, hearings, invoices, and lawyer assignments efficiently.

Business Requirements

  • Client Management: Maintain a comprehensive database of clients, including contact details and active lawsuits.
  • Lawsuit Management: Track lawsuits with their hearings and status updates.
  • Hearing Scheduling: Schedule and manage hearings for lawsuits.
  • Invoice Management: Generate and track invoices for legal services.
  • Lawyer Assignment: Assign lawyers dynamically and view current assignments.
  • Entity Relationship Diagram (ERD): Visual representation of the database schema.
  • Data Validation: Use field-level validation to ensure data integrity.
  • Error Handling: Provide meaningful error messages to users.

Core Features

Manage client information, including personal details and associated lawsuits. Functions include creating, reading, updating, and deleting client records.

Centralized system to manage lawsuits. Supports creating, reading, updating, and deleting lawsuit records.

Schedule and manage hearings related to lawsuits. Full CRUD functionality included.

Manage legal service invoices with capabilities to create, update, and track payments.

Assign lawyers to lawsuits and view current assignments. Full management functionality provided.
